# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Czech homeowners (35–60) planning to enclose or upgrade their residential property. They arrive via Google or word of mouth, comparing 2–3 fencing companies. Their job: evaluate professionalism, quality, and trustworthiness before requesting a free quote. Mobile-first visit, final decision on desktop after reviewing the portfolio.

## Product Purpose

Konstanta HP manufactures and installs custom aluminum fencing, gates, wicket gates, and bioclimatic pergolas across the Czech Republic. They design, fabricate, and install — no subcontractors, full control of quality. Success means the visitor requests a free quote (Kalkulace zdarma) after the first scroll.

## Brand Personality

Modern · Local · Trustworthy. A Czech craft manufacturer that takes every job personally. Not corporate, not flashy. The brand should feel like a reliable neighbor who happens to be exceptionally good at what they do.

## Anti-references

- Current site: generic white contractor look with gray accents and no personality
- Cream / warm-neutral beige backgrounds (saturated AI default of 2026)
- Glossy SaaS / tech visual language (gradients, glassmorphism, hero metrics)
- Heavy editorial / display-serif aesthetic (Vogue-style, broadsheet grid)

## Design Principles

1. Craft confidence over corporate polish — the work speaks, the design supports it
2. Every section earns its presence — no eyebrow-on-every-heading scaffolding
3. The product is aluminum, not pixels — let photography carry the visual weight
4. One voice, not a template — sections diverge in treatment when the content demands it
5. Accessible to all visitors — WCAG AA minimum, Czech language, clear CTA hierarchy

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Czech language (cs). Prefers-reduced-motion supported throughout. Clear reading hierarchy for older users. Sufficient contrast on all text.
